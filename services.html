How We Work

Our Engagement Process

01
Discovery

We begin with a thorough requirements and scope discussion, presenting available solution options aligned to your goals and constraints.

02
Proposal

Technology selection, timeline, budget, and team composition are defined, documented, and shared with full transparency before engagement begins.

03
Resourcing

The agreed team is assembled based on required skillsets and member availability, the right people for your exact challenge.

04
Engagement

Regular update meetings and milestones keep you informed throughout delivery, with a formal project handoff at successful completion.

Flexible Delivery

Engagement Models

Choose the model that fits your team, timeline, and budget, or blend them to suit evolving needs.

On Demand Team

Temporary consultants and technical specialists handle defined-scope projects with clear timelines. Ideal for one-time initiatives, peak capacity needs, or exploratory projects without long-term commitment.

Dedicated Team

A persistent Rapdfly delivery team replaces the overhead of internal hiring for ongoing BAU work. Long-term, deeply embedded, and fully aligned with your operations and culture.

Hybrid Model

Selected team members work on-site for deep process understanding while others support remotely, combining the intimacy of embedded teams with the flexibility of distributed delivery.

Consulting

Industry experts develop strategies and roadmaps that address your most complex business challenges. Pure advisory, no implementation dependency. Ideal for strategic inflection points and transformation planning.
