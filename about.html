Our Story

From a bold idea
to a thriving force

Rapdfly was founded in 2018 with a clear and ambitious vision, to become the most reliable value-adding partner for businesses navigating a rapidly evolving digital landscape. What began as a consulting play in e-commerce and technology implementations quickly grew into something far bigger.

Our founders recognised that businesses weren't just searching for software vendors, they needed a trusted co-pilot. Someone who understood their goals, spoke their language, and had the technical depth to turn strategy into reality. That insight became the DNA of Rapdfly.

Today we operate across sales & marketing, branding, CRM, web development, AI, IoT, and enterprise systems, serving clients in manufacturing, banking, retail, and beyond. Every engagement is anchored in one principle: add real value, every time.
