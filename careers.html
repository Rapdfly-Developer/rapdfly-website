Backend Developer
Full-Time Fresher & Experienced AI Advantage

Design and build the server-side logic that powers our enterprise platforms. You'll architect APIs, manage databases, and ensure the scalability and security of systems used by large organisations.

  • Strong foundation in Python, Node.js, Java, or similar backend language
  • Experience with RESTful API design and implementation
  • Proficiency with relational databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL) or NoSQL
  • Understanding of cloud services, AWS, Azure, or GCP
  • Knowledge of authentication, security, and data protection principles
  • Familiarity with microservices or service-oriented architecture is a plus

Project Manager
Full-Time Fresher & Experienced AI Advantage

Lead end-to-end delivery of enterprise IT projects, from kickoff to go-live. You'll coordinate cross-functional teams, manage client expectations, and ensure every project is delivered on time, on budget, and above expectations.

  • Strong organisational, planning, and stakeholder communication skills
  • Familiarity with project management methodologies (Agile, Scrum, Waterfall)
