Who We Are

Rapdfly, Value Adding Service Provider

Founded in 2018, Rapdfly was built on a single conviction: technology should work for people, not the other way around. We partner with enterprises across manufacturing, banking, healthcare, and retail to design, build, and run IT systems that genuinely move the needle.

From our base in HSR Layout, Bangalore, our team of engineers, consultants, and strategists has delivered 50+ enterprise solutions spanning AI, IoT, ERP, CRM, and beyond.
